# My Signature / 我的特调

## 故事 / Story
One or two paragraphs: origin, mood, or the idea behind the drink. Bilingual is ideal.

## 配方 / Ingredients
| 用量 Amount | 材料 Ingredient |
| --- | --- |
| 50 ml | Gin 金酒 |
| 25 ml | Fresh lemon juice 鲜柠檬汁 |
| 15 ml | Simple syrup 糖浆 |

## 步骤 / Steps
1. Chill a coupe glass with ice water; set aside.
2. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
3. Shake hard for 12–15 seconds.
4. Double-strain into the chilled glass.
5. Express the lemon twist oils over the surface and drop it in.
